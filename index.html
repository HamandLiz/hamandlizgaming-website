The Duo

Ham and Liz are a married couple—a duo through and through. As parents to two amazing kiddos, they are parents by day, but once the kids go to bed, the gamers come out! They love diving into whatever game catches their interest at the time. From spreading democracy in Helldivers 2 and surviving blood moons in 7 Days to Die, to Palworld, American Truck Sim, Golf with Friends, Repo, Fortnite, and more—they have played just about everything.

They run this massive broadcast on a custom 3-PC setup: two dedicated gaming rigs and one powerhouse streaming PC that runs the whole show. But they don't just spend time inside! They frequently take the show on the road for IRL streams, sharing their outdoor adventures with the community using their custom mobile streaming setup, the Mushbox.

Ham

Ham is a husband, father, gamer, and IT professional. Ham has been gaming since he was a little kid, starting out on the NES and progressing through Sega, PlayStation, Xbox, and PC. Always fascinated by technology, he took an early liking to computers and electronics. With over 20 years of IT experience, he loves talking tech on stream—whether it's the stream setup, his home network, or day-to-day work. His gaming legacy is vast, with favorites ranging from Doctor Mario on the NES and Sonic on Sega to Kingdom Hearts on PlayStation and Halo on Xbox.

Liz

The creative force and the voice of reason (usually). Keeps the base organized, snipes enemies from afar, and brings the artistic flair to the channel.
